Data Appendings

Through data appending, we enhance your database by updating outdated or missing data, ensuring it remains accurate and comprehensive.

  • Data Arrangement and Uploading: After identifying the data for appending, we convert it into CSV format and combine it into a single file, which is uploaded to the provider’s secure FTP. The files are then sent to the appending team.
  • Matching and Enhancing: When the data reaches the appending team, it undergoes detailed formatting. The client’s file is aligned with the master business records, and once a match is made between the name, address, and email, the data is immediately appended.
  • Verification and Validation: Following the matching process, the appended files are handed over to the verification team for thorough validation, with each email being verified manually and automatically

Benefits of Data Appending for Your Business

In today’s fast-paced business environment, data is the cornerstone of success. Accurate, up-to-date information enables businesses to make informed decisions, connect with the right customers, and stay ahead of competitors. However, many businesses struggle with incomplete or outdated data, which can lead to missed opportunities and inefficient marketing efforts. This is where data appending comes into play. Data appending enhances your existing database by adding missing information or correcting inaccuracies.
